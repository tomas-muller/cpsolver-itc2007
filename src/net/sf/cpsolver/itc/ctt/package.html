Track 3: Curriculum based Course Timetabling

The Curriculum-based timetabling problem consists of the weekly scheduling of the lectures for several university courses within a given number of rooms and time periods, where conflicts between courses are set according to the curricula published by the University and not on the basis of enrollment data.

See http://www.cs.qub.ac.uk/itc2007/curriculmcourse/course_curriculm_index.htm for more information about this track.
